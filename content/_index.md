### Experience

**MIT Media Lab** — *Ph.D. Researcher* (2022-2025)
Research spanning NLP, AI alignment, network science, and computational social science. Built AI voice agents for policy preference collection, developed tools for collective decision-making, co-developed ConGraT (contrastive pre-training for joint graph and text embeddings), and conducted large-scale studies of language polarization on social networks.

**Amazon AGI Foundations** — *Applied Scientist Intern* (Summer 2024)
Introduced improvements to alignment methods for Amazon's Titan model, including curriculum learning and novel loss functions, leading to improvements on benchmarks.

**Mana AI** — *ML Research Intern* (Summer 2022)
Built the founder- and company-identification engine using graph neural networks and NLP on LinkedIn and Twitter data to surface investment opportunities.

**Bridgewater Associates** — *Researcher and Engineer* (2017-2020)
Founding member of ML team at world's largest hedge fund. Built machine-learning pipeline for trading systems. Mentored by Dr. Jasjeet Sekhon (now Chief Strategy Officer at Google DeepMind).
